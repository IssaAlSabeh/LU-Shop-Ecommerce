# LU Shop Ecommerce Website

## About LU Shop

LU Shop is your one-stop destination for all your shopping needs. Whether you're looking for the latest fashion trends, electronics, home decor, or more, we've got you covered. Our user-friendly ecommerce platform is designed to provide a seamless and enjoyable shopping experience for our customers.

## Website Pages

### 1. Homepage/Products

The homepage is the first page visitors see when they land on our website. It serves as the gateway to our extensive product catalog. Here's what you can expect on the Homepage/Products page:

- **Product Catalog:** Browse through our wide range of products, including the latest fashion trends, electronics, home decor, and more.

- **Product Details:** Click on a product to view its details, including images, description snf pricing.

- **Add to Cart:** Easily add products to your cart directly from the product details page.


### 2. My Cart

The My Cart page is where you can review and manage the items you've added to your shopping cart. Here's what you can do on this page:

- **View Cart Contents:** See a list of items you've added to your cart, including product details, quantities, and prices.

- **Update Quantities:** Easily adjust the quantity of each item or remove products from your cart.

- **Proceed to Checkout:** When you're ready to complete your purchase, click the "Proceed to Checkout" button to finalize your order.

### 3. About Us

Our "About Us" page provides insights into who we are as a company and what sets us apart. Here's what you can find on this page:

- **Company Overview:** Learn about our mission, values, and commitment to customer satisfaction.

## Getting Started

How to install the project:

1. Open local host xampp or wampp
2. Turn on apache and mysql
3. Create database named mydb
4. Open mysql server and apply the query inside file 'mydb.sql'
5. move the LU_Shop folder into your www or htdocs folder
